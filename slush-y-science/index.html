Slush Y Science
Project Manager

The idea of Slush Y Science 2017 event was to bring forth the value of science-based business, and encourage scientists to embrace a different way of thinking about making an impact. The event was an official vertical of Slush and attracted more than 800 participants. I was responsible for orchestrating the whole operative part of the event. The event was hold on December 2017 in Helsinki.
