Helsinki Robot Uprising
Responsible for Concept Design and Social Media Operations
Helsinki Robot Uprising was the greatest 48 hour robot hackathon race in Finland in 2017. It was a real-world, hands-on engineering and design challenge. The hackathon gathered together 120 participants. It was a completely voluntary-based project to boost awareness in robotics. We creatively combined theatre in the original robotics hackathon concept and got into Finnish news. I was a key part of the team designing the concept and taking care of social media.

We built a twisted race track with five brain-racking challenges. The hackathon participants had to design, build and program Lego Mindstorms robots to pass these trials as fast and as well as possible .See the video on the right for better wow-factor. Robot Uprising project was an international success. We got participants all the way from Egypt to Helsinki to take part in the uprising.
