Timeout
Junior Advisor, Finnish Innovation Fund

Timeout (Erätauko) is part of the Next Era initiative launched by Sitra and Demos Helsinki. The purpose of the Timeout is to strengthen people’s participation in society and understanding of political decision-making. I was member of the working group to create vision, plan and test different methods, together with local participants and the media. We were particularly interested in engaging people who do not typically participate in these kinds of events or feel uncomfortable taking part in public debate.

Pilot project

Our team organised an experiment, "Tulevaisuuden kyselytunti" in Finnish Parliament Annex, where we invited ministers and welcomed citizens. It was a Q&A session in which ministers answered questions of the citizens present – instead of Members of Parliament. Normally the Finnish parliament has a questioning sessions only for the members of parliament. We aimed to improve dialogue between political decision-makers and citizens. Based on the citizens' feedback, our test project succeed strengthening citizens' feeling of participation in political process.
