What I Do
People like me are called interdisciplinary designers. In short it is when you tend to be curious about many things in life and eventually end up knowing a lot across several disciplines.
In Detail
I'm coming from a Social Sciences background, with a multidisciplinary twist, where I developed relevant research and communication skills. Master of Social Sciences helped me to excell understanding of the qualitative research processes. Minoring in Economics and Computer Science helped me to excell in structural thinking. While moving further along my career path working as a concept designer in a Internet of Things startup and then an academic program producer I enhanced those skills with design thinking methods and driving innovation as well as attention to the project management side of the entities I create.
